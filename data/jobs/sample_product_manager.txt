Job Title: Senior Product Manager – AI / ML Products
Company: InnovateTech
Location: Hybrid (New York / Remote)
Type: Full-Time

ABOUT THE ROLE
InnovateTech is seeking a Senior Product Manager to lead development of AI-powered product features. You will define strategy, write PRDs, work with engineering and design teams, and deliver products that delight users and drive business growth.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Define product vision, strategy, and roadmap for AI/ML-powered features
- Write detailed product requirements documents (PRDs), user stories, and acceptance criteria
- Conduct user research, interviews, and usability studies to deeply understand customer needs
- Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, and designers in agile sprint planning
- Define and track KPIs, OKRs, and success metrics using analytics tools
- Manage stakeholder communication and executive reporting
- Lead A/B experiments and interpret results to drive product decisions
- Prioritise backlog items using frameworks like RICE, MoSCoW, and Kano
- Coordinate with marketing and sales for product launches and go-to-market strategy
- Stay current with AI/ML trends and identify opportunities for product innovation

REQUIRED SKILLS & QUALIFICATIONS
- 5+ years of product management experience, including 2+ years on AI/ML products
- Proven track record of shipping successful B2B or B2C products
- Strong analytical skills: SQL, Amplitude, Mixpanel, or Tableau for data analysis
- Experience with agile methodologies: Scrum, Kanban
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Experience writing PRDs, user stories, and functional specifications
- Comfort with technical concepts: APIs, ML pipelines, data infrastructure
- Experience running A/B tests and interpreting statistical results
- Proficiency with product tools: JIRA, Confluence, Figma, Notion
- Strong stakeholder management and cross-functional leadership skills

NICE TO HAVE
- Technical background (Computer Science, Data Science, or Engineering degree)
- Experience with LLM-based products, chatbots, or recommendation engines
- MBA or equivalent advanced degree
- Experience at a high-growth startup or FAANG company
- Certification in product management (PMPO, Pragmatic Institute)

TECH STACK & TOOLS
JIRA, Confluence, Figma, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Tableau, SQL, Notion, Slack, Zoom

COMPENSATION
- Salary: $150,000 – $200,000 USD
- Annual bonus: 15–20%
- Equity, health benefits, 401(k), unlimited PTO
